About Gon

On a beautiful island, Whale, was born Gon Freeks. Growing up immensely connected with nature, and developing extraordinary finesse with everyday tools he chose his weapon to be a simple fishing rod! Ever since he was young he was curious about his absent father,Ging, causing him to become a Hunter himself in his quest to search for his world renowned hunter dad. He's only twelve when he appears for the baffling exam and wins the heart of Killua, a deadly assassin, impacted by Gon's humility and spirit. What sets him apart truly is he's astoundingly selfless when helping others no matter what the challenge may be.
